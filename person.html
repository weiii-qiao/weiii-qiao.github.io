About / Publication / Experience / Resume

I am an incoming Ph.D. student at Xidian University, where I will be advised by Prof. Teng Li in Prof. Jianfeng Ma's group. Currently, I am a Research Assistant in Prof. Yang Liu's group at Nanyang Technological University (NTU).

I am completing my Master's degree at the Institute of Information Engineering, Chinese Academy of Sciences (IIE, CAS), advised by Assoc. Prof. Bo Jiang in Prof. Zhigang Lu's team. I hold a B.E. in Software Engineering from Xidian University.

My research primarily focuses on cybersecurity, specifically:

  • Host-based Intrusion Detection
  • APT Provenance
  • LLM Agents for Security
